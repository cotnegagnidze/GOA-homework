About Mario

Since his first appearance in 1981, Mario has become a cultural icon and the most beloved video game character of all time.

Mario is known for his adventures in the Mushroom Kingdom, saving Princess Peach from the infamous Bowser, and his incredible athletic abilities—running, jumping, and collecting power-ups.

With countless games, spin-offs, and his signature "It's-a me, Mario!" voice line, Mario has inspired generations and remains a symbol of joy and creativity.

Dynamic Gameplay

From platformers to sports games, Mario keeps players entertained with innovative adventures.

Iconic Power-Ups

Mushrooms, stars, and fire flowers—Mario's arsenal makes every challenge fun!

Beloved Friends

Luigi, Yoshi, and Princess Peach—Mario's companions make his story unforgettable.
